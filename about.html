About Us

A café built on one simple idea

We just wanted a place we'd actually want to spend time in.

Our story

Most cafés are designed to move people through quickly. Bean & Brew was designed around the opposite idea — that a good cup of coffee deserves more than ninety seconds of your attention, and so does the place you drink it in.

Everything we serve comes from somewhere we can actually point to on a map. Our beans are roasted twenty minutes away by a small family operation we've worked with since day one. The pastries arrive before 7am from a bakery two streets over. Even the honey in our lattes has a beekeeper behind it — one we visit every spring.

Over the years, Bean & Brew has quietly become a lot of things to a lot of people. A second office for freelancers. A Tuesday ritual for the retired couple from the corner flat. A first date. A dissertation deadline. We don't try to be all of those things — people just seem to find what they need here, and that suits us fine.

2019

Opened our doors

Twelve seats, one espresso machine, and a lot of optimism.

2020

Survived the hard part

Takeaway only for months. Our regulars kept us going.

2022

First events programme

Started hosting workshops, tastings, and live music nights.

2024

Extended the space

Added a back room for events and a proper window seat.

What we stand for

A few things we care about enough to actually do something about.

🌱

Local sourcing

We know where everything comes from - the beans, the flour, the honey. If we can't trace it, we don't serve it.

♻️

Low waste

Compostable packaging throughout. Everything unsold at closing goes to a local food bank, every single day.

🤝

Fair pay

All staff are paid above the living wage. Every tip goes directly and entirely to the team — no exceptions.

☕

Quality over speed

We'd rather make it right than make it fast. If there's a short wait, it's because your drink is worth it.

Come say hello

We're always happy to hear from people - whether you have a question, a suggestion, or just want to know if we do oat milk. We do. Come find us on High Street, or drop us a message.
